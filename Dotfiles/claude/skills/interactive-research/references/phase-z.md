# Phase Z — narrow to one conclusion

> Load this when you judge the research sufficient, or when a single-answer Phase 0 lets you skip straight here. The notes-log format and certainty markers are defined in the root `SKILL.md`.

Synthesize supporting material from the turn notes (not from a re-dump of raw sources) and the human's direction/responses into `conclusion.md`; ensure you understand all their arguments, and aren't under-weighting something they mentioned in passing in favour of a body-of-research that was siloed into an irrelevant direction:

- the **answer / conclusion**, stated plainly, up front
- the reasoning, with **certainty markers** (+SURE / ~SUSPECT / -GUESS / --WONDER) on each load-bearing claim
- the **competing options and why-not** — show the frontier you narrowed across, for later re-weighing against evolving priorities
- **citations** throughout
- the **quarantine list** — the separated discoveries, as their own section for the human to triage (file as TODOs/tasks if that fits the project)

Put the most important content first and last; that's where your attention, and theirs, is strongest.

Before delivering, run `validate.sh` one final time (see *Tooling* in `SKILL.md`) — `conclusion.md`'s citations are the load-bearing ones, so every bracketed slug in it must resolve to a registered source.
